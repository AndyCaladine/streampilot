Privacy & Data

Last updated May 2026 — StreamPilot Beta

What we collect
We collect only what we need to run StreamPilot. This includes:
  • Your name and email address to create and manage your account
  • Your chosen display name shown on your dashboard
  • Your Twitch profile data (display name, avatar, channel ID) via the Twitch API
  • Your StreamPilot settings and preferences
Twitch connection
StreamPilot connects to your Twitch account using the official Twitch API and OAuth. We request only the permissions needed to run your dashboard. You can disconnect Twitch at any time from Settings → Connections.
UK GDPR & Data Protection
Your data is stored and processed in the United Kingdom under the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018. You have the right to erasure — contact us via Settings to request deletion within 30 days.
