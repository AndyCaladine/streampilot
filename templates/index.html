I got tired of juggling 10+ tools just to run a broadcast. Overlays here, alerts there, chat somewhere else — nothing talking to anything else. I’m not a developer who read a Reddit thread about streamers. I stream. I live this. StreamPilot is the tool I always wished existed, and I’m building it properly so other streamers don’t have to feel the same frustration.

Everything in one place

Stop juggling tabs. StreamPilot gives you everything you need to run a professional stream from a single dashboard.

Live Dashboard

Viewers, followers, subscribers and uptime at a glance. World clocks for your global audience.

Chat Management

Monitor and moderate your chat in real time. Commands, filters and moderation tools built in.

Stream Alerts

Beautiful follow, sub, raid and bits alerts sent directly to your OBS overlay.

Timed Panels

Schedule information panels to appear on screen automatically throughout your stream.

Team Tools

Invite mods and co-pilots. Everyone gets the right access for their role.

Desktop App

Install StreamPilot as a desktop app. No browser tabs, no distractions. Just your cockpit.
