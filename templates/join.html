Privacy & Data

Last updated May 2026 — StreamPilot Beta

What we collect
We collect only what we need to run StreamPilot. During beta this includes:
  • Your name and email address to create and manage your account
  • Your chosen display name shown on your dashboard
  • Your Twitch profile data (display name, avatar, channel ID) via the Twitch API
  • Stream activity data — viewer counts, follows, subscriptions and raids — to power your dashboard
  • Your StreamPilot settings and preferences
Why we collect it
Account Your name and email let you sign in and recover your account.
Dashboard Twitch data powers your live stats, alerts and stream tools.
Experience Preferences are saved so your setup is consistent across sessions.
Safety Activity logs help us debug issues and protect your account.
Twitch connection
StreamPilot connects to your Twitch account using the official Twitch API and OAuth. We request only the permissions needed to run your dashboard. We never post to your channel, send messages, or take actions without your instruction. You can disconnect Twitch at any time from Settings → Connections — this removes the link but keeps your StreamPilot account. To delete your account entirely, contact us from Settings.
UK GDPR & Data Protection
Your data is stored and processed in the United Kingdom. StreamPilot operates under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), as retained in UK law by the Data Protection Act 2018. We are committed to handling your personal data lawfully, fairly, and transparently.

Under UK GDPR you have the right to erasure — also known as the right to be forgotten. If you would like us to delete your account and all associated personal data, please use the Contact Us form in the StreamPilot settings, or reach out via the official beta Discord channel. We will action all erasure requests within 30 days.
Your rights
You can request a copy of your data, correct inaccuracies, or request deletion at any time. During beta, contact us via the feedback link in the dashboard. We aim to respond within 7 days.
